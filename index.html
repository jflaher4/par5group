PARTNERS
TOM CLARK
Prior to joining Par 5 Group, Tom spent 12 years in South Florida working for Executive Sports International. In 2002 Tom joined the PGA TOUR as Executive Director of the Ford Senior Players Championship in Dearborn, Michigan. In 2005 Tom was appointed as Executive Director of the 2007 Presidents Cup at Royal Montreal and 2009 Presidents Cup at Harding Park in San Francisco. Following the 2009 Presidents Cup Tom was the Executive Director for the 2010 and 2011 Charles Schwab Cup Championships at Harding Park. Tom was transferred to Atlanta in 2012 to become The Executive Director of the TOUR Championship at East Lake Golf Club in Atlanta. Tom spent 6 years in Atlanta before moving to the PGA TOUR headquarters in Ponte Vedra Beach.
TIM FLAHERTY
As a former basketball coach Tim believes strongly in commitment, discipline and execution, principles essential to managing multi-faceted spectator events. He spent 20 years at the United States Golf Association where he was Senior Director, U.S. Senior Open and U.S. Women’s Open Championships. After graduating from Amherst College he played basketball in Ireland and helped create the Belfast United program which fostered cross-community relations in Northern Ireland.
BRIAN FLAJOLE
Brian Flajole has been a key organizer in many of the top sporting events— the two U.S. Olympic Trials, the Amgen Tour of California bicycle race, the AVP Pro Beach Volleyball Tour and for a decade, the Longs Drugs Challenge LPGA tournament. Now he’s championship director of the 2015 U.S. Senior Open, which will make its Northern California debut at Del Paso Country Club June 22-28, 2015. Brian also managed and oversaw the Boeing Classic an official event on the PGA Tour Champions.
DOUGLAS HABGOOD
Douglas has great passion for marketing and staging events to provide new and unique experiences for all audiences. Since graduating from the University of South Carolina, he has been immersed in every aspect of international professional golf management. Armed with a creative mind and fun-loving mentality, Douglas welcomes every opportunity to engage with talented teams to deliver groundbreaking features to high-profile athletic competitions.
SEAN SOVACOOL
With more than 30 years managing professional golf events, Sovacool has been a part of teams that managed 27 USGA Championships and more than 150 events across the LPGA Tour, PGA TOUR, PGA TOUR Champions, and Korn Ferry Tour. He began his career working for Executive Sports, a division of Golden Bear, holding positions in Operations, Marketing, Sales, and overall Tournament Management. In 2001, he joined Bruno Event Team, where he managed the Golf Division for more than 20 years. Regularly managing ten events annually across all Tours.
